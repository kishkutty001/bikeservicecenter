Privacy Policy - BikeServiceCenter

Last updated: September 2025

Welcome to BikeServiceCenter. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services:

  • What data we collect and why
  • How your data is stored and secured
  • Your rights regarding your data
  • How to contact us for concerns

1. Data We Collect

1.1 User Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Location details (pickup/drop-off, service location)
  • Vehicle details (model, registration number)

1.2 Service Data

  • Requests for scheduled or in-place services
  • Assigned mechanic details (for tracking and updates)
  • Service status and history

1.3 Payment Data

  • Transaction details (via secure payment gateways)
  • Invoices and receipts

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Allow secure login and account access
  • Enable booking of scheduled or in-place services
  • Allow admin to assign mechanics
  • Provide real-time service updates
  • Generate invoices and process payments securely

3. Data Security

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure Firebase/Database authentication
  • HTTPS communication for all requests
  • Role-based access (admin, mechanic, customer)

4. Your Rights

  • View and update your profile anytime
  • Request data deletion
  • Delete your account permanently
  • Opt out of optional notifications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication & cloud storage)
  • Google Maps API (for live location tracking)
  • Payment gateways (for secure transactions)
  • Analytics tools (for app improvement)

All providers are required to handle your data securely and responsibly.

6. Children's Privacy

BikeServiceCenter is not designed for children under 13. If we detect such data, it will be deleted promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. All changes will be reflected in-app and on our website.
